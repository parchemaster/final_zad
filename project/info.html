Here you can find a guide for gameplay

For PC you can use WASD/Arrows or Drag&Drop/Click to move your ship. "Space" key or Mouse click for shooting. "Esc" key for pause menu.

For Mobile you can use Drag&Drop/Gyroscope to move your ship. Touch(tap) for shooting.

To unlock new level you have to finish all quests for previous one.
